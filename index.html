How to use CoinLabs

  1. Navigate to "Create Token" from the menu.
  2. Fill in your token's name, symbol, and upload a cool image.
  3. Configure decimals (e.g., 9 for meme coins) and total supply.
  4. Add an optional description for your token.
  5. Review all details. If everything looks good, proceed to mint.
  6. You'll be prompted to send the minting fee to our minting address.
  7. Once payment is confirmed, your tokens are minted to your wallet!
The cost of creating a Token is 0.1 SOL. This includes all fees for SPL Token creation. The process is quick, and you'll receive the total supply.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is CoinLabs

CoinLabs is a user-friendly platform allowing easy creation of Solana-based SPL tokens without coding, plus tools to explore trending coins.

How do I create a token?

Go to "Create Token", fill the form (name, symbol, image, decimals, supply), review, and click "Mint Token". You'll then be prompted for the payment.

What are the fees?

Token creation costs 0.1 SOL (base fee, advanced options may add to this). Creating a liquidity pool costs 0.2 SOL. Pomote tokens costs range from 0.25 SOL to 0.4 SOL depending on choice selected. These fees cover network transactions and platform services.
